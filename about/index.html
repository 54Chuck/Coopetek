About Coopetek

Professional Quality Management Service Provider

Who We Are

Coopetek is a professional quality management service provider with four laboratories in mainland China, located in Shanghai, Wuhu (Anhui), Pingshan (Shenzhen), and Bao'an (Shenzhen). We provide reliable testing, inspection, audit and training services to our clients.

We have over 100 professional inspectors, auditors, and consultants operating across multiple regions including mainland China (Guangzhou, Dongguan, Shenzhen, Xiamen, Hangzhou, Ningbo, Suzhou, Changzhou, Qingdao, Jinan, Wuhan, Wuhu), Taiwan (Taipei and Kaohsiung), as well as Japan, Korea, Vietnam, Malaysia, Cambodia, and India.
