Testing Services

Comprehensive testing solutions for toys, textiles, and electronics

Overview

Our testing services provide comprehensive analysis and verification across three major industries: Toys & Hardgoods, Textiles & Footwear, and Electronics & Electrical Appliances. We ensure your products meet international safety standards and regulatory requirements for global markets.

Our Testing Capabilities:

Toys & Hardgoods

Physical and mechanical safety testing, chemical analysis, flammability testing, and age grading assessment for toys and children's products.

Textiles & Footwear

Color fastness, fiber content analysis, physical testing, restricted substances testing, and performance evaluation for textiles and footwear.

Electronics & Electrical

Safety testing, EMC testing, energy efficiency testing, and performance testing for electronic and electrical products.

Chemical Analysis

Testing for restricted substances, heavy metals, phthalates, and other chemical compounds according to international regulations.
